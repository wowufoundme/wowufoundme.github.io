The First
Sep 29, 2019

It was the first day of Physics practical and I was already fifteen minutes late, practically running with a heavy bag full of books. I rushed to Block 2 of my college building and then ran up the stairs to the third floor which was apparently very quiet. The only thing I saw at the end of the hall was a shadow of a girl standing outside the lab with a bag on her arms. I probably didn't recognise her at that very moment. In my mind I had already assumed that she was told to stand outside for being late and that the very same thing is going to happen to me. I literally just stoop straight up right there thinking of all the possibilites of what could happen now.

I walked up to her. She was my classmate, although at that time, I didn't really knew her name. To be honest, for a moment I forgot how pretty she was. *ahem, ahem*
"Hey", I said while heavily breathing in and out.
"Hi", she replied.
"Is Mrs. Sen in already?", I asked.
"Yeah."
"Oh shit! She's gonna kill me for being this late. What did she say to you?"
"Nothing! I didn't even go in."
"Wait... What?"

Okay. Now was probably a good time to maybe just go inside, give this a try, talk to the teacher, but no.
